Philippine Folk Dances


Dance Description Island

CARINOSA 		Known as the national dance of the Philippines
, Carinosa is a flirtatious courtship dance with participants using
fans and handkerchiefs as props.		 Luzon

KURATSA 		A lively courtship dance from the
Visayas region, characterized by playful and
flirtatious movements between partners.		 Visayas

PANTOMINA 		Known as the “Dance of the Doves,”
Pantomina simulates the courtship rituals of birds,
with dancers imitating the male bird’s pursuit of his mate. 		Visayas

SINGKIL 		A mesmerizing dance that tells the
story of a Maranao princess and her suitor,
featuring precise footwork as dancers
gracefully avoid clashing bamboo poles.		 Mindanao

TINIKLING 		This famous dance mimics the movements
of the tikling bird, as dancers skillfully navigate between
bamboo poles that are rhythmically tapped together. 		Luzon
